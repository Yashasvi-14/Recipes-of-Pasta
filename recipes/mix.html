Mixed Sauce Pasta



Description:

Mixed Sauce Pasta is a perfect fusion of creamy white sauce and tangy red sauce. This dish combines the richness of Béchamel sauce with the bold flavors of tomato sauce, making it an irresistible choice for pasta lovers. It includes garlic, herbs, cheese, and vegetables for a well-balanced and flavorful meal.

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta (penne, fusilli, or macaroni)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper powder
  • ½ teaspoon oregano or mixed Italian herbs
  • ½ teaspoon chili flakes
  • ¼ cup grated cheese
  • ½ cup tomato puree (from 2-3 tomatoes)
  • 1 teaspoon chopped garlic
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup chopped vegetables (bell peppers, mushrooms, corn, or zucchini)

Steps:

  1. Boil 4 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add salt, oil, and pasta; cook for 7-10 minutes.
  3. Drain and rinse the pasta with cold water.
  4. Heat butter in a pan, add flour, and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Slowly add milk, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Add black pepper, chili flakes, oregano, and cheese. Cook until thick.
  7. In another pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and onions.
  8. Add tomato puree, salt, and cook until thickened.
  9. Combine the white sauce and red sauce together.
  10. Add the cooked pasta and mix well.
  11. Cook for 1-2 minutes, garnish, and serve hot.

Red Sauce Pasta



Description:

Red Sauce Pasta is a flavorful Italian dish made with a tangy tomato-based sauce. The sauce is prepared using fresh tomatoes, garlic, onions, and Italian herbs, giving the pasta a rich and delicious taste. It is perfect for a comforting and hearty meal.

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta (penne, fusilli, or macaroni)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chopped garlic
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 large tomatoes, blended into puree
  • ½ teaspoon chili flakes
  • ½ teaspoon oregano or mixed Italian herbs
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ cup grated cheese (optional)
  • ½ cup chopped vegetables (bell peppers, mushrooms, or zucchini)

Steps:

  1. Boil 4 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add salt, oil, and pasta; cook for 7-10 minutes.
  3. Drain and rinse the pasta with cold water.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onions.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until thick.
  6. Season with chili flakes, oregano, black pepper, and salt.
  7. Cook vegetables in the sauce for a few minutes.
  8. Mix pasta with the red sauce and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  9. Garnish with grated cheese and serve hot.
